Cats: A Poem!

With paws so soft,
on silent feet,

They pounce and play,
in mischief meet.

Under moonlight,
whiskers sway.

In cardboard boxes, they plot and play

Tails flick, in joy they leap,

In the world of cats,

fun never sleeps.
